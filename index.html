About us

Welcome to Euphory: Your Online Learning Experience At Euphory, we believe in the power of hands-on learning. Our small online school is dedicated to equipping students with the skills they need to succeed in the real world. With a focus on project-based learning, we help you not only understand theoretical concepts but also apply them by building tangible projects. Our Unique Learning Model Real Projects At Euphory, you won’t just be learning from textbooks; you will engage in real projects that reflect the latest industry standards. Our courses are designed to provide you with practical experience, ensuring that you can showcase your skills to potential employers or clients. Post-Course Opportunities Once you complete your course, we don’t stop there. We will upload your project to our app, My Business Platform, where you can gain exposure and potentially generate income. If your project is running or on sale, Euphory receives 20% of the total revenue it generates. This model not only incentivizes you to create high-quality work but also provides ongoing support as you transition into the market. Meet Your Instructor Your learning journey will be guided by Ms. Erica, our experienced teacher based in Australia. With a wealth of knowledge and a passion for teaching, Ms. Erica is committed to helping each student reach their full potential. Her hands-on approach ensures that you receive personalized feedback and support throughout your learning experience. Enrollment Process Getting started at Euphory is easy! To enroll, simply send us an email, and we will send you a form to complete your registration. Once enrolled, you will receive your timetable and login details to access our online platform.
